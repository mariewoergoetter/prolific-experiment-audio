You will listen to some sentences.

Your task is to indicate which continuation, A or B, sounds more natural given the sentence you heard.

Move the slider toward the continuation that sounds more natural. The further you move the slider to one side, the more natural that continuation sounds compared with the other.

Please complete the experiment in a quiet environment.

The experiment takes approximately 10 minutes. We will start with two examples.

Legal Information

Your participation in this study is voluntary. PURPOSE OF RESEARCH STUDY
The goal of the project is to evaluate the language abilities of AI models.

PROCEDURES
On each page, you will listen to a sentence and read two possible continuations. Your task is to indicate which continuation sounds more natural given the sentence you heard.

RISKS/DISCOMFORTS
The risks associated with participation in this study are no greater than those encountered in daily life.

BENEFITS
There are no direct benefits to you from participating in this study. This study may benefit society if the results lead to a better understanding of the limits of modern technology.
