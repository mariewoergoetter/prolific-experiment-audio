You will read some sentences. Your task is to indicate which continuation (A or B) sounds more natural to you, given the stated sentence.

Use the slider to move toward the continuation that fits better in your opinion. The closer you move the slider toward one side, the stronger your preference for that continuation.

The experiment takes about 7 minutes. We’ll start with two examples.

Legal Information

Your participation in this study is voluntary. PURPOSE OF RESEARCH STUDY
The goal of the project is to evaluate the language abilities of AI models.

PROCEDURES
On each page, you will read a sentence and two possible continuations. Your task is to choose which one of the two continuations sounds more natural.

RISKS/DISCOMFORTS
The risks associated with participation in this study are no greater than those encountered in daily life.

BENEFITS
There are no direct benefits to you from participating in this study. This study may benefit society if the results lead to a better understanding of the limits of modern technology.
